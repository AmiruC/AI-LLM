How to enable Low Power Mode
Go to Settings → Battery → Toggle on Low Power Mode. This reduces background activity and extends battery life.

How to backup your iPhone to iCloud
Go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → iCloud Backup → Tap “Back Up Now.” Make sure you're connected to Wi-Fi.

How to change your default browser
Go to Settings → Scroll to your browser (e.g., Chrome or Firefox) → Tap “Default Browser App” → Select your preferred option.

How to set up Face ID
Go to Settings → Face ID & Passcode → Tap “Set up Face ID” and follow on-screen instructions to register your face.

How to share your Wi-Fi password
If your device is unlocked and near the recipient, a prompt will appear to share your Wi-Fi password automatically. Both devices must be running iOS 11 or later.
