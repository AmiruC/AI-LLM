iPhone 15 Pro
Features a titanium body, A17 Pro chip, USB-C port, and ProMotion 120Hz display. Available in 6.1-inch and 6.7-inch (Pro Max) sizes.

iPhone 15
Comes with the A16 Bionic chip, Dynamic Island, and a new 48MP main camera. Available in 6.1-inch and 6.7-inch Plus sizes.

iPhone SE (3rd generation)
A budget-friendly option with a Home button and Touch ID. Powered by the A15 Bionic chip and has a 4.7-inch display.

Color Options
iPhone 15 Pro comes in natural titanium, blue titanium, white titanium, and black titanium. iPhone 15 comes in pink, yellow, green, blue, and black.

Storage Capacities
iPhone 15 Pro and Pro Max: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, 1TB. iPhone 15: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB. iPhone SE: 64GB, 128GB, 256GB.
